Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur
Arrival: Reach Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Kalyan).
    Visit the City Palace and Jantar Mantar.
    Stroll around Hawa Mahal and Johari Bazaar.
Stay: Hotel Kalyan, Jaipur

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing
Activities:
    Explore Amber Fort and enjoy an elephant ride.
    Visit Jal Mahal and Nahargarh Fort.
    Shop at Bapu Bazaar for traditional Rajasthani items.
Stay: Hotel Kalyan, Jaipur

Day 3: Jaipur to Pushkar (140 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Pushkar.
    Visit the Brahma Temple and the sacred Pushkar Lake.
    Experience the local culture at the Pushkar Bazaar.
Stay: Hotel New Park, Pushkar

Day 4: Pushkar to Udaipur (280 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Early morning departure for Udaipur.
    En route, stop at Ajmer to visit the Ajmer Sharif Dargah.
    Check into a hotel (e.g., Hotel Udai Palace).
    Evening boat ride on Lake Pichola.
Stay: Hotel Udai Palace, Udaipur

Day 5: Udaipur Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyon Ki Bari.
    Explore the Bagore Ki Haveli museum.
    Attend a cultural dance show in the evening.
    Stay: Hotel Udai Palace, Udaipur

Day 6: Udaipur to Mount Abu (165 km, 3.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Mount Abu, Rajasthan's only hill station.
    Visit the Dilwara Temples and Nakki Lake.
    Enjoy the sunset at Sunset Point.
Stay: Hotel Hillock, Mount Abu

Day 7: Mount Abu to Jodhpur (270 km, 5.5 hours)
Activities:
    Depart for Jodhpur.
    Check into a hotel (e.g., The Ummed).
    Visit the Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada.
Stay: The Ummed, Jodhpur

Day 8: Jodhpur Sightseeing
Activities:
    Explore Umaid Bhawan Palace and Mandore Gardens.
    Shop at Clock Tower Market.
    Visit the Rao Jodha Desert Rock Park.
Stay: The Ummed, Jodhpur

Day 9: Jodhpur to Jaisalmer (285 km, 5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Jaisalmer, the Golden City.
    Check into a hotel (e.g., Hotel Golden Haveli).
    Visit the Jaisalmer Fort and Patwon Ki Haveli.
Stay: Hotel Golden Haveli, Jaisalmer

Day 10: Jaisalmer Desert Experience
Activities:
    Explore the Thar Desert with a camel safari.
    Visit Sam Sand Dunes and enjoy a Rajasthani cultural program.
    Stay overnight in a desert camp.
Stay: Desert Camp, Jaisalmer

Day 11: Jaisalmer to Bikaner (330 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Bikaner.
    Visit the Junagarh Fort and Karni Mata Temple (Rat Temple).
    Check into a hotel (e.g., Hotel Sagar).
Stay: Hotel Sagar, Bikaner

Day 12: Bikaner to Jaipur (330 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Jaipur.
    Visit Galtaji Temple (Monkey Temple) before departure.
    Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.
