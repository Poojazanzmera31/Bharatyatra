12-Day Bihar and Jharkhand Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Patna, Bihar
Arrival: Reach Patna, the capital city of Bihar, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Maurya).
    Visit Golghar, Patna Museum, and Gandhi Maidan.
    Enjoy an evening stroll by the Ganges River.
Stay: Hotel Maurya, Patna

Day 2: Patna to Nalanda and Rajgir (90 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Nalanda and explore the ancient Nalanda University ruins and Nalanda Archaeological Museum.
    Continue to Rajgir and visit Venu Vana, the Griddhakuta Hill, and Japanese Peace Pagoda.
Stay: Hotel Siddharth, Rajgir

Day 3: Rajgir to Bodh Gaya (75 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Bodh Gaya, the place where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.
    Visit the Mahabodhi Temple, Bodhi Tree, and various international monasteries.
    Participate in an evening prayer at the temple.
Stay: Hotel Bodhgaya Regency, Bodh Gaya

Day 4: Bodh Gaya to Gaya and Back (15 km, 30 minutes)
Activities:
    Drive to Gaya and visit Vishnupad Temple, Mangla Gauri Temple, and Pretshila Hill.
    Return to Bodh Gaya for relaxation and meditation.
Stay: Hotel Bodhgaya Regency, Bodh Gaya

Day 5: Bodh Gaya to Deoghar, Jharkhand (200 km, 5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Deoghar, known for its spiritual significance.
    Visit the Baidyanath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.
    Explore the Nandan Pahar and Trikut Hill.
Stay: Hotel Yashoda International, Deoghar

Day 6: Deoghar to Ranchi (270 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand.
    Visit the Tagore Hill, Ranchi Lake, and Pahari Mandir.
    Enjoy the local Jharkhand cuisine.
Stay: Hotel Radisson Blu, Ranchi

Day 7: Ranchi Sightseeing
    Activities:
    Visit the Birsa Zoological Park, Rock Garden, and Kanke Dam.
    Explore the beautiful waterfalls around Ranchi, such as Hundru Falls and Jonha Falls.
Stay: Hotel Radisson Blu, Ranchi

Day 8: Ranchi to Jamshedpur (130 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Jamshedpur, known as the Steel City.
    Visit the Tata Steel Zoological Park, Jubilee Park, and Dimna Lake.
Stay: Hotel Fortune Park Centre Point, Jamshedpur

Day 9: Jamshedpur to Hazaribagh (180 km, 4.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Hazaribagh, a city surrounded by forests and natural beauty.
    Visit Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary and Canary Hill.
    Explore the local markets.
Stay: Hotel Canary Hill, Hazaribagh

Day 10: Hazaribagh to Bhagalpur (200 km, 5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Bhagalpur, known for its silk industry.
    Visit the Vikramshila Ruins, Mandar Hill, and Kuppaghat Ashram.
Stay: Hotel Rajhans International, Bhagalpur

Day 11: Bhagalpur to Vaishali and Back to Patna (200 km, 5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Vaishali, an important archaeological site related to Buddha.
    Visit Ashokan Pillar, Vishwa Shanti Stupa, and the Relic Stupa.
    Return to Patna for the night.
Stay: Hotel Maurya, Patna

Day 12: Departure from Patna
Activities:
    Last-minute shopping or relaxing.
    Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.

Key Highlights and Famous Things in Bihar and Jharkhand
Mahabodhi Temple (Bodh Gaya): A UNESCO World Heritage Site where Buddha attained enlightenment.
Nalanda University: Ancient center of learning and a significant historical site.
Baidyanath Temple (Deoghar): A major pilgrimage site for Hindus, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
Ranchi Waterfalls: Picturesque waterfalls that are popular tourist attractions in Jharkhand.
