12-Day West Bengal Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata
Arrival: Reach Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., The Peerless Inn).
    Visit the iconic Victoria Memorial and St. Paul's Cathedral.
    Explore the Indian Museum, one of the oldest museums in India.
Stay: The Peerless Inn, Kolkata.

Day 2: Kolkata Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the Howrah Bridge and Dakshineswar Kali Temple.
    Explore the Marble Palace and Tagore House (Jorasanko Thakur Bari).
    Enjoy a traditional Bengali dinner at a local restaurant.
Stay: The Peerless Inn, Kolkata.

Day 3: Kolkata to Sundarbans (110 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Sundarbans, the largest mangrove forest in the world.
    Check into a forest lodge and enjoy a boat safari in the Sundarbans National Park.
    Evening cultural program showcasing local folklore.
Stay: Sundarban Tiger Camp.

Day 4: Sundarbans Wildlife Safari
Activities:
    Full-day wildlife safari exploring the creeks and spotting Bengal tigers, crocodiles, and various bird species.
    Visit a local village to experience the rural lifestyle of the Sundarbans.
Stay: Sundarban Tiger Camp.

Day 5: Sundarbans to Shantiniketan (160 km, 4 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Shantiniketan, the town founded by Rabindranath Tagore.
    Visit the Visva-Bharati University campus and Rabindra Bhavan.
    Explore the local handicrafts and artwork at Amar Kutir.
Stay: Hotel Royal Bengal, Shantiniketan.

Day 6: Shantiniketan to Bishnupur (100 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Bishnupur, famous for its terracotta temples.
    Visit the Rasmancha, Jor Bangla Temple, and Madan Mohan Temple.
    Explore the local markets for Baluchari sarees and terracotta artifacts.
Stay: Hotel Annapurna, Bishnupur.

Day 7: Bishnupur to Darjeeling (580 km, overnight train)
Activities:
    Board an overnight train to Darjeeling.
    Enjoy a scenic journey through the rolling hills and tea gardens of West Bengal.
Stay: Overnight train journey.

Day 8: Darjeeling Sightseeing
Activities:
    Arrive in Darjeeling and check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Mayfair Darjeeling).
    Visit Tiger Hill for a stunning sunrise view of the Kanchenjunga range.
    Explore the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park.
Stay: Mayfair Darjeeling.

Day 9: Darjeeling Tea Garden Tour
Activities:
    Visit a local tea garden and factory to learn about the tea production process.
    Explore the Darjeeling Mall Road and shop for local handicrafts.
    Ride the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train).
Stay: Mayfair Darjeeling.

Day 10: Darjeeling to Kalimpong (50 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Kalimpong, a serene hill station.
    Visit Deolo Hill, Durpin Monastery, and the Cactus Nursery.
    Explore local markets for Tibetan handicrafts and souvenirs.
Stay: The Elgin Silver Oaks, Kalimpong.

Day 11: Kalimpong to Kolkata (600 km, overnight train)
Activities:
    Drive back to New Jalpaiguri Railway Station for an overnight train to Kolkata.
Stay: Overnight train journey.

Day 12: Departure from Kolkata
Activities:
    Arrive in Kolkata and enjoy last-minute shopping or relaxing.
    Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.

Key Highlights and Famous Things in West Bengal
    Victoria Memorial: A symbol of British India's architectural splendor.
    Sundarbans National Park: The largest mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
    Darjeeling: Known for its tea gardens and breathtaking views of the Himalayas.
    Shantiniketan: A cultural and educational hub founded by Rabindranath Tagore.
    Bishnupur: Renowned for its unique terracotta temples and traditional Baluchari sarees.
