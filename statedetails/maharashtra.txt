12-Day Maharashtra Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Mumbai
Arrival: Reach Mumbai, the financial capital of India, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Suba Palace).
    Visit the Gateway of India and Marine Drive.
    Explore Colaba Causeway for shopping and street food.
Stay: Hotel Suba Palace, Mumbai

Day 2: Mumbai Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the Elephanta Caves (a ferry ride from the Gateway of India).
    Explore the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (a UNESCO World Heritage Site).
    Visit Haji Ali Dargah and Bandra-Worli Sea Link.
Stay: Hotel Suba Palace, Mumbai

Day 3: Mumbai to Pune (150 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Pune.
    Visit the Aga Khan Palace and Shaniwarwada Fort.
    Explore Osho International Meditation Resort.
Stay: Hotel Aurora Towers, Pune

Day 4: Pune to Mahabaleshwar (120 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Mahabaleshwar.
    Visit Venna Lake and Pratapgad Fort.
    Explore Mapro Garden for fresh strawberries and local products.
Stay: Evershine Resort, Mahabaleshwar

Day 5: Mahabaleshwar Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit Elephant's Head Point and Arthur's Seat.
    Enjoy a trek to Wilson Point for panoramic views.
    Explore local markets for handicrafts.
Stay: Evershine Resort, Mahabaleshwar

Day 6: Mahabaleshwar to Alibaug (120 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Alibaug.
    Visit Alibaug Beach and Kolaba Fort.
    Relax at Kihim Beach or Nagaon Beach.
Stay: U Tropicana Alibaug, Alibaug

Day 7: Alibaug to Lonavala (100 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Lonavala.
    Visit Bhushi Dam and Lonavala Lake.
    Explore the Karla Caves and Bhairoba Temple.
    Stay: Fariyas Resort, Lonavala

Day 8: Lonavala Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit Della Adventure Park for some adventure activities.
    Explore the Tiger’s Leap and Duke’s Nose for scenic views.
    Relax at Khandala and enjoy local cuisine.
Stay: Fariyas Resort, Lonavala

Day 9: Lonavala to Nashik (200 km, 4 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Nashik.
    Visit Sula Vineyards for wine tasting.
    Explore Kalaram Temple and Panchavati.
    Stay: The Gateway Hotel, Nashik

Day 10: Nashik to Aurangabad (220 km, 4.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Aurangabad.
    Visit the Ellora Caves and Ajanta Caves (a UNESCO World Heritage Site).
    Explore Bibi Ka Maqbara.
Stay: Hotel Aurangabad Gymkhana, Aurangabad

Day 11: Aurangabad to Mumbai (340 km, 6.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Mumbai.
    Visit Chor Bazaar for antiques and Sanjay Gandhi National Park.
    Explore Juhu Beach for a final taste of Mumbai’s street food.
    Stay: Hotel Suba Palace, Mumbai

Day 12: Departure
Activities:
Depending on your flight/train schedule, you can visit Prince of Wales Museum or relax in a local café.
Departure from Mumbai.
Key Highlights and Famous Things in Maharashtra
Gateway of India: An iconic monument and historical landmark in Mumbai.
Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures on Elephanta Island.
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its Victorian Gothic architecture.
Sula Vineyards: Renowned for wine production and tasting in Nashik.
Ajanta and Ellora Caves: UNESCO World Heritage Sites known for their intricate cave temples and sculptures.
Mahabaleshwar: Famous for its scenic viewpoints and strawberry farms.
