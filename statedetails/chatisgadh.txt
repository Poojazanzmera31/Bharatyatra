12-Day Chhattisgarh Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Raipur
Arrival: Reach Raipur by flight or train. 
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Babylon International).
    Visit the Mahant Ghasidas Memorial Museum and Rajiv Smriti Van.
    Explore local markets and enjoy a traditional Chhattisgarhi meal. 
Stay: Hotel Babylon International, Raipur

Day 2: Raipur Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the Nandan Van Zoo and Safari.
    Explore the Vivekananda Sarovar and Purkhauti Muktangan.
    Enjoy local cuisine at a renowned restaurant. 
Stay: Hotel Babylon International, Raipur

Day 3: Raipur to Bilaspur (110 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Bilaspur.
    Visit the Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary.
    Explore the local markets and temples. 
Stay: Hotel Dwarika, Bilaspur

Day 4: Bilaspur to Korba (120 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Korba.
    Visit the Koriya and Koriya Wildlife Sanctuaries.
    Explore local attractions and markets. 
Stay: Hotel Grand Korba, Korba

Day 5: Korba to Jagdalpur (250 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Jagdalpur.
    Visit the Chitrakote Waterfall and Tirathgarh Waterfall.
    Explore the local tribal markets. 
Stay: Hotel Mangi's, Jagdalpur

Day 6: Jagdalpur Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the Kanger Valley National Park and its famous caves (Kanger Caves).
    Explore local tribal villages and learn about their culture.
    Enjoy traditional tribal cuisine. 
Stay: Hotel Mangi's, Jagdalpur

Day 7: Jagdalpur to Dantewada (90 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Dantewada.
    Visit the Dantewada Temple and the local market.
    Explore the nearby village and interact with locals. 
Stay: Hotel Anand, Dantewada

Day 8: Dantewada to Raigarh (220 km, 5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Raigarh.
    Visit the Raigarh Fort and the local market.
    Explore nearby attractions and enjoy local cuisine. 
Stay: Hotel Arpit, Raigarh

Day 9: Raigarh to Bilaspur (150 km, 3.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Bilaspur.
    Free day for leisure activities or revisiting favorite spots. 
Stay: Hotel Dwarika, Bilaspur

Day 10: Bilaspur to Raipur (110 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Raipur.
    Visit any missed attractions or explore local markets. 
Stay: Hotel Babylon International, Raipur

Day 11: Raipur to Sirpur (80 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Sirpur.
    Visit the Sirpur Archaeological Site and Laxman Temple.
    Explore the local area and return to Raipur. 
Stay: Hotel Babylon International, Raipur

Day 12: Departure from Raipur
Activities:
    Last-minute shopping or relaxing.
    Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.

Key Highlights and Famous Things in Chhattisgarh
    Chitrakote Waterfall: Often called the "Niagara of India," it's one of the largest waterfalls in India.
    Kanger Valley National Park: Known for its scenic beauty and biodiversity.
    Sirpur Archaeological Site: Famous for its ancient temples and ruins.
