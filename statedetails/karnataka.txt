12-Day Karnataka Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bengaluru
Arrival: Reach Bengaluru, the capital city of Karnataka, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Chancery Pavilion).
    Visit Lalbagh Botanical Garden and Bangalore Palace.
    Explore the bustling MG Road and Commercial Street.
Stay: Hotel Chancery Pavilion, Bengaluru

Day 2: Bengaluru Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit ISKCON Temple and Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace.
    Explore Cubbon Park and Vidhana Soudha.
    Enjoy traditional Kannadiga cuisine at a local restaurant.
Stay: Hotel Chancery Pavilion, Bengaluru

Day 3: Bengaluru to Mysuru (150 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Mysuru.
    Visit the Mysore Palace and St. Philomena's Church.
    Explore the Mysuru Zoo and Brindavan Gardens in the evening.
Stay: Hotel Sandesh The Prince, Mysuru

Day 4: Mysuru to Coorg (120 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Coorg (Kodagu).
    Visit Abbey Falls and Raja's Seat for scenic views.
    Explore the coffee plantations and Dubare Elephant Camp.
Stay: Hotel Coorg International, Coorg

Day 5: Coorg Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit Talakaveri, the origin of the River Kaveri.
    Explore Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple) in Bylakuppe.
    Enjoy a nature walk in the coffee plantations.
Stay: Hotel Coorg International, Coorg

Day 6: Coorg to Chikmagalur (150 km, 3.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Chikmagalur.
    Visit Mullayanagiri, the highest peak in Karnataka.
    Explore the coffee estates and Baba Budangiri.
Stay: The Serai, Chikmagalur

Day 7: Chikmagalur to Hampi (320 km, 7 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Hampi.
    Visit the Virupaksha Temple and Vittala Temple.
    Explore the ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.
Stay: Hampi’s Boulders Resort, Hampi

Day 8: Hampi Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the Lotus Mahal and Queen's Bath.
    Explore the Elephant Stables and Hazara Rama Temple.
    Take a coracle ride on the Tungabhadra River.
Stay: Hampi’s Boulders Resort, Hampi

Day 9: Hampi to Badami (140 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Badami.
    Visit the Badami Cave Temples and Agastya Lake.
    Explore the Bhutanatha Temples and Badami Fort.
Stay: Hotel Clarks Inn, Badami

Day 10: Badami to Gokarna (250 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Gokarna.
    Visit Om Beach and Kudle Beach.
    Explore the Mahabaleshwar Temple.
    Stay: Sanskruti Resort, Gokarna

Day 11: Gokarna to Udupi (150 km, 3.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Udupi.
    Visit the Udupi Sri Krishna Matha and Malpe Beach.
    Explore St. Mary’s Island.
Stay: Paradise Isle Beach Resort, 

Day 12: Departure from Mangaluru
Activities:
    Drive to Mangaluru (60 km, 1.5 hours).
    Visit the Kadri Manjunath Temple and Mangaladevi Temple.
    Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.

Key Highlights and Famous Things in Karnataka
    Mysore Palace: A stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture, one of India's most famous palaces.
    Hampi: A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its ancient temples and ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.
    Coorg: Famous for its coffee plantations, lush landscapes, and the origin of the Kaveri River.
