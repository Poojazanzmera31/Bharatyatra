12-Day Delhi Travel Itinerary
Day 1: Arrival in Delhi
Arrival: Reach Delhi, the capital of India, by flight or train.
Activities:
Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Connaught).
Visit the Red Fort and Jama Masjid.
Explore Chandni Chowk for street food and shopping.
Stay: Hotel Connaught, Delhi
Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing (Old Delhi)
Activities:
Visit the Raj Ghat (Mahatma Gandhi’s memorial).
Explore Humayun's Tomb.
Walk around India Gate and Rashtrapati Bhavan.
Stay: Hotel Connaught, Delhi
Day 3: Delhi Sightseeing (New Delhi)
Activities:
Visit Qutub Minar and Lotus Temple.
Explore the National Museum and National Gallery of Modern Art.
Shop at Connaught Place.
Stay: Hotel Connaught, Delhi
Day 4: Delhi to Agra (230 km, 4 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Agra.
Visit the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort.
Explore the Mehtab Bagh for a sunset view of the Taj.
Stay: Hotel Taj View, Agra
Day 5: Agra to Jaipur (240 km, 5 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Jaipur.
Check into a hotel (e.g., Hotel Kalyan).
Visit the City Palace and Jantar Mantar.
Stay: Hotel Kalyan, Jaipur
Day 6: Jaipur Sightseeing
Activities:
Explore Amber Fort and enjoy an elephant ride.
Visit Jal Mahal and Nahargarh Fort.
Shop at Bapu Bazaar.
Stay: Hotel Kalyan, Jaipur
Day 7: Jaipur to Pushkar (140 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Pushkar.
Visit the Brahma Temple and Pushkar Lake.
Experience the local culture at Pushkar Bazaar.
Stay: Hotel New Park, Pushkar
Day 8: Pushkar to Udaipur (280 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Udaipur.
Check into a hotel (e.g., Hotel Udai Palace).
Enjoy an evening boat ride on Lake Pichola.
Stay: Hotel Udai Palace, Udaipur
Day 9: Udaipur Sightseeing
Activities:
Visit the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyon Ki Bari.
Explore the Bagore Ki Haveli museum.
Attend a cultural dance show in the evening.
Stay: Hotel Udai Palace, Udaipur
Day 10: Udaipur to Mount Abu (165 km, 3.5 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Mount Abu.
Visit the Dilwara Temples and Nakki Lake.
Enjoy the sunset at Sunset Point.
Stay: Hotel Hillock, Mount Abu
Day 11: Mount Abu to Jodhpur (270 km, 5.5 hours)
Activities:
Drive to Jodhpur.
Check into a hotel (e.g., The Ummed).
Visit the Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada.
Stay: The Ummed, Jodhpur
Day 12: Jodhpur to Delhi (660 km, 10 hours)
Activities:
Drive back to Delhi.
Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.
Key Highlights and Famous Things in Delhi
Red Fort: Iconic Mughal fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Jama Masjid: One of the largest mosques in India.
India Gate: War memorial and popular landmark.
Qutub Minar: Ancient minaret and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Lotus Temple: A Bahá'í House of Worship known for its lotus-shaped design.
