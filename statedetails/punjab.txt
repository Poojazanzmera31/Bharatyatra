12-Day Punjab Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Amritsar
Arrival: Reach Amritsar, the spiritual and cultural hub of Punjab, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel (e.g., Hotel Ramada).
    Visit the Golden Temple and Jallianwala Bagh.
    Explore the Wagah Border for the evening flag-lowering ceremony.
Stay: Hotel Ramada, Amritsar

Day 2: Amritsar Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit the Partition Museum and Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum.
    Explore Akal Takht and Gobindgarh Fort.
    Enjoy traditional Punjabi cuisine at a local restaurant.
Stay: Hotel Ramada, Amritsar

Day 3: Amritsar to Jalandhar (90 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Jalandhar.
    Visit the Devi Talab Mandir and Rangla Punjab.
    Explore the local markets for traditional handicrafts.
Stay: Hotel Suba Palace, Jalandhar

Day 4: Jalandhar to Ludhiana (75 km, 1.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Ludhiana.
    Visit the Punjab Agricultural University Museum and Ludhiana Museum.
    Explore Raja Dhiraj Palace and the local markets.
Stay: Hotel Park Plaza, Ludhiana

Day 5: Ludhiana to Patiala (80 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Patiala.
    Visit the Qila Mubarak, Sheesh Mahal, and Moti Bagh Palace.
    Enjoy a stroll in Baradari Garden.
Stay: Hotel Patiala, Patiala

Day 6: Patiala to Chandigarh (80 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Chandigarh.
    Visit Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, and Chandigarh Rose Garden.
    Explore Sector 17 Plaza for shopping.
Stay: Hotel Shivalik View, Chandigarh

Day 7: Chandigarh to Mohali (20 km, 30 minutes)
Activities:
    Drive to Mohali.
    Visit the Mohali Cricket Stadium and Punjab Cricket Association Stadium.
    Explore local parks and markets.
Stay: Hotel Cama, Mohali

Day 8: Mohali to Amritsar (120 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Amritsar.
    Free day for leisure activities or shopping.
Stay: Hotel Ramada, Amritsar

Day 9: Amritsar to Kapurthala (90 km, 2 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Kapurthala.
    Visit the Jagatjit Palace, Kapurthala Cathedral, and Shalimar Gardens.
    Explore local markets.
Stay: Hotel D.S., Kapurthala

Day 10: Kapurthala to Faridkot (110 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Faridkot.
    Visit the Qila Mubarak, Gurdwara Faridkot Sahib, and Faridkot Fort.
    Explore local attractions and markets.
Stay: Hotel MBD Radisson, Faridkot

Day 11: Faridkot to Amritsar (150 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Amritsar.
    Leisure day for shopping or revisiting favorite spots.
Stay: Hotel Ramada, Amritsar

Day 12: Departure from Amritsar
Activities:
    Last-minute shopping or relaxing.
    Drop off at the airport or railway station for your return journey.

Key Highlights and Famous Things in Punjab
    Golden Temple: The holiest Sikh shrine and a symbol of peace and harmony.
    Jallianwala Bagh: Historic site of the 1919 massacre.
    Wagah Border: Famous for the evening flag-lowering ceremony.
    Patiala: Known for its palaces, including Qila Mubarak and Sheesh Mahal.
    Chandigarh: Modern city designed by Le Corbusier, featuring Rock Garden and Sukhna Lake.
