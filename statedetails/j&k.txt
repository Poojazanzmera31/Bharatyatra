12-Day Jammu & Kashmir Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
Arrival: Reach Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir, by flight or train.
Activities:
    Check into a mid-range hotel or houseboat (e.g., Houseboat New Shaheen).
    Visit the Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.
    Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
Stay: Houseboat New Shaheen, Srinagar

Day 2: Srinagar Sightseeing
Activities:
    Explore the Old City: Visit Jama Masjid and Shankaracharya Temple.
    Visit Hari Parbat Fort for panoramic views of Srinagar.
    Explore Lal Chowk for local shopping and cuisine.
Stay: Houseboat New Shaheen, Srinagar

Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg (50 km, 1.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Gulmarg, the “Meadow of Flowers”.
    Visit the Gulmarg Gondola (one of the highest cable cars in the world).
    Explore St. Mary’s Church and enjoy nature walks.
Stay: Hotel Hilltop, Gulmarg

Day 4: Gulmarg Sightseeing
Activities:
    Visit Apharwat Peak via the Gondola ride.
    Enjoy a round of golf at the Gulmarg Golf Course (seasonal).
    Explore Khilanmarg and enjoy scenic views.
Stay: Hotel Hilltop, Gulmarg

Day 5: Gulmarg to Pahalgam (95 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Pahalgam, a picturesque town on the banks of the Lidder River.
    Visit Aru Valley and Betaab Valley for stunning landscapes.
    Enjoy a river rafting experience or a pony ride.
Stay: Hotel Hilltop, Pahalgam

Day 6: Pahalgam Sightseeing
Activities:
    Explore Baisaran (Mini Switzerland) and Pahalgam’s Local Market.
    Visit Mamal Temple and Lidder River.
Stay: Hotel Hilltop, Pahalgam

Day 7: Pahalgam to Jammu (290 km, 6 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Jammu, the winter capital of Jammu & Kashmir.
    Visit the Vaishno Devi Temple (if time permits).
    Explore Raghunath Temple and Ranbireshwar Temple.
Stay: Hotel Jammu Ashok, Jammu

Day 8: Jammu to Katra (50 km, 1.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Katra.
    Visit the Vaishno Devi Temple (trek up to the temple; it may take a full day or more).
Stay: Hotel The White, Katra

Day 9: Katra to Patnitop (110 km, 3 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Patnitop, known for its lush green meadows.
    Visit Naag Mandir and Sanasar Lake.
    Enjoy a peaceful walk or picnic in the meadows.
Stay: Hotel Patnitop Heights, Patnitop

Day 10: Patnitop to Srinagar (190 km, 4.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Srinagar.
    Visit any remaining sites or enjoy leisure time on Dal Lake.
Stay: Houseboat New Shaheen, Srinagar

Day 11: Srinagar to Sonamarg (80 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive to Sonamarg, the “Meadow of Gold”.
    Explore Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy scenic views.
    Enjoy a local meal and nature walks.
Stay: Hotel Sonamarg Glacier, Sonamarg

Day 12: Sonamarg to Srinagar (80 km, 2.5 hours)
Activities:
    Drive back to Srinagar.
    Depending on your departure schedule, visit Nigeen Lake or relax in a local café.
    Departure from Srinagar.

Key Highlights and Famous Things in Jammu & Kashmir
    Dal Lake: Known for its houseboats and Shikara rides.
    Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi are exquisite examples of Mughal landscaping.
    Gulmarg Gondola: One of the highest cable cars in the world with panoramic views.
    Vaishno Devi Temple: A major pilgrimage site located in Katra.
    Aru Valley and Betaab Valley: Beautiful landscapes and outdoor activities.
