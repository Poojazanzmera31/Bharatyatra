    Activities:

    Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing
    Activities:
        Explore India Gate, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb.
        Visit Lotus Temple and drive past Rashtrapati Bhavan.
        Shop at Connaught Place and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.
        Stay: Hotel Hari Piorko, Delhi

    Day 3: Delhi to Agra (230 km, 4 hours)
    Activities:
        Early morning departure for Agra.
        Visit the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort.
        Explore Mehtab Bagh for a sunset view of the Taj Mahal.
    Stay: Hotel Taj Resorts, Agra

    Day 4: Agra to Mathura-Vrindavan to Fatehpur Sikri (100 km, 2 hours)
    Activities:

    Stay: Hotel Goverdhan Palace, Mathura

    Day 5: Mathura to Lucknow (360 km, 6 hours)
    Activities:
        Depart for Lucknow, the City of Nawabs.

    Stay: Hotel Clarks Avadh, Lucknow

    Day 6: Lucknow Sightseeing
    Activities:
        Visit Rumi Darwaza and the British Residency.
        Stroll around Hazratganj Market for shopping and local cuisine.
        Visit the Ambedkar Memorial Park.
    Stay: Hotel Clarks Avadh, Lucknow

    Day 7: Lucknow to Varanasi (320 km, 5.5 hours)
    Activities:
        Early morning departure for Varanasi.

    Stay: Hotel Alka, Varanasi

    Day 8: Varanasi Sightseeing
    Activities:
        Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple and Sankat Mochan Temple.
